a. Matrix A 7x9, rank 5 has 5 independent columns and 5 in dependent rows
-> dim C(A) = 5, dim N(A) = 9-5 = 4
-> dim C($A^T$) = 5, dim N($A^T$) = 7-5 = 2

dim C(A) + dim N(A) + dim C($A^T$) + dim N($A^T$) = 5 + 4 + 5 + 2 = 16 = m + N
